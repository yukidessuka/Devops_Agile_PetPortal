Nutrition Advice For

Your Pets

Understanding how much and what to feed your pets can play a key role in maintaining a healthy weight, as well as knowing how many treats they’re allowed. Read our advice below on the right diet for your dog, cat, rabbit.

Nutrition Advice For Dogs

A balanced diet with protein, healthy fats, and carbs keeps dogs healthy. Avoid overfeeding to prevent obesity. Never feed chocolate, grapes, or onions.

Nutrition Advice For Cats

Cats need high animal protein and taurine for heart and eye health. Provide wet food for hydration. Avoid dog food, cow’s milk, and raw fish.

Nutrition Advice For Rabbits

Rabbits require a high-fiber diet, with hay being the primary food source. Supplement with leafy greens and avoid sugary fruits.

Nutrition Advice For Birds

Birds require a varied diet that includes seeds, fruits, and vegetables to meet their nutritional needs. Avoid chocolate, caffeine, and avocado.

Nutrition Advice For Ducks

Ducks need a balanced diet of grains, vegetables, and protein. Avoid bread as it lacks nutrients and can lead to malnutrition.

Nutrition Advice For Hamsters

Hamsters thrive on a diet rich in protein and healthy fats. Avoid sugary treats or processed foods, as they can lead to obesity and diabetes.
